Benjamin Britton Photography

Benjamin Britton is a wedding and engagement photographer from northeast Ohio. This slick custom site, built with React.js, showcases his special artistic sensibilities and unique personality within an easily navigable framework that allows potential customers to easily view his work, check out his pricing packages, read his blog, or contact him for a session!

Background

For this project, I was tasked with creating a beautiful and highly functional website for Mr. Britton's photography business. My main goal while building the site was to create a final product that captured his clean, artistic aesthetic and quirky personality while, above all, making sure to showcase his work wherever possible. I wanted to make sure that any potential customers that visit his site are not only wowed by his work, but really get a sense for who he is, as wedding photography can be such a personal experience. I also strived to make the site as clear and as easily-navigable as possible, with links to his portfolio, pricing, social media pages, and most importantly, the contact page all over the site.

Throughout the process, I worked closely with Mr. Britton to make sure I incorporated all of the elements he desired in his site, including a personal blog, which I built with the help of Sanity Studio. I also created a logo for Mr. Britton for branding purposes, as can be seen on the left side of the site navbar.

The site was built with React.js and styles were implemented with the help of Tailwind CSS, which made it easy to create a beautiful, responsive design that looks great on all screen sizes, from the tiniest cell phone to 2XL monitors.
