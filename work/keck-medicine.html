Keck Medicine of USC

WordPress Enterprise Web Application & Full Website Redesign

Summary

Role: Lead Developer (2+ years)

Live Site: keckmedicine.org

Technologies: WordPress, Gutenberg, ACF, Yoast SEO, Azure, Google Maps API, Pantheon Solr

Led full website redesign as a member of a 3-person development team, consolidating 23 sub-sites into one fully-customized WordPress enterprise instance serving approximately 1 million users per month. This massive undertaking vastly simplified their technical architecture while significantly increasing website performance and SEO to outperform local competition in the LA area.

Key Contributions & Development

Custom Search & Location Tools

Built custom Find a Location and Find a Provider search tools with Google Maps integration, allowing patients to easily locate healthcare facilities and medical professionals. These tools significantly improved user experience and helped drive business to the Keck Medicine health system.

Global Search Implementation

Implemented custom global search functionality using Pantheon Solr, providing users with powerful search capabilities across the entire site content. This dramatically improved content discoverability and user engagement.

Comprehensive Template Library

Built out extensive template system including:

  • Service Lines - Standardized templates for medical service offerings
  • Centers and Programs - Specialized templates for healthcare centers and programs
  • Conditions/Treatments - Medical condition and treatment information pages
  • Blogs - Healthcare blog and news content
  • Keck Medicine Magazine - Digital magazine content
  • Custom Events Post Type - Healthcare events and seminars

Technical Leadership & Mentoring

Mentored and led a team of client developers, working with clients on a daily basis in an agile environment. Provided training sessions and established internal WordPress expertise within the client organization over the course of 2 years.

Project Impact & Results

Enterprise Scale Results

Successfully delivered a healthcare website serving ~1 million users per month, consolidating 23 sub-sites into one unified platform. The performance improvements and SEO optimization helped Keck Medicine outrank LA-area healthcare competition, directly driving business growth for the health system.

  • Enterprise WordPress application serving 1M+ monthly users
  • Consolidated 23 sub-sites into unified architecture
  • Custom Google Maps integration for location services
  • Advanced Solr search implementation
  • Comprehensive template library for healthcare content
  • SEO optimization outperforming local competition
  • 2+ years of ongoing development and maintenance
