Luminis Health

Post-Launch Stabilization & Feature Development

Summary

Role: Senior Developer (1+ year)

Live Site: luminishealth.org

Technologies: Drupal, Twig, PHP, JavaScript, SCSS, jQuery, Acquia, Bitbucket

Supported post-launch stabilization and feature development for enterprise healthcare site. Worked in agile environment addressing high-priority bugs and implementing new features including MyChart integration with custom dashboard and innovative Find Care tool that guides users through personalized flows to easily schedule appointments with care providers.

Project Context & Challenges

Post-Launch Critical Period

High-Stakes Environment: Joined the project immediately after a hectic and expedited launch, inheriting a site that was mostly developed but still contained numerous high-priority bugs and challenges that needed immediate attention. The product was now live and in active use by patients and healthcare providers, making rapid issue resolution critical.

Agile Problem-Solving

Rapid Response Development: Worked in an agile environment alongside the lead developer to quickly identify, prioritize, and resolve issues while simultaneously implementing new features to enhance the platform's functionality and user experience.

Key Contributions & Features

MyChart Integration

Healthcare System Integration: Supported the development and refinement of MyChart integration with a custom dashboard, enabling patients to access their medical records, appointments, and healthcare information directly through the Luminis Health website.

Innovative Find Care Tool

Personalized User Experience: Played a key role in building an innovative Find Care tool that revolutionizes how patients discover and connect with healthcare providers. The tool features:

  • Personalized User Flow: Guides users through highly personalized paths based on their specific treatment needs and preferences
  • Treatment-Specific Guidance: Tailors recommendations based on the type of care or treatment the user is seeking
  • Flexible Scheduling: Enables users to easily schedule both in-person and video appointments with their chosen care providers
  • Provider Matching: Connects patients with the most appropriate healthcare professionals for their specific needs

Critical Infrastructure Stabilization

Platform Reliability: Focused extensively on cleaning up and optimizing the functionality and styles of core site features including Find Care, Find a Doctor, and Find a Location tools, ensuring reliable performance during the critical early months of the site's lifecycle.

Impact & Results

Critical Launch Support

Successful Stabilization: Successfully stabilized a complex enterprise healthcare platform during its most vulnerable period immediately post-launch. Ensured that critical functionality remained operational while implementing improvements and new features.

Enhanced User Experience

The Find Care tool and MyChart integration significantly improved the patient experience, making it easier than ever for users to find appropriate healthcare providers, access their medical information, and schedule appointments through multiple modalities.

Technical Achievements

  • Rapid bug resolution during critical post-launch period
  • Seamless MyChart integration with custom dashboard
  • Advanced Find Care tool with personalized user flows
  • Multi-modal appointment scheduling (in-person and video)
  • Improved Find a Doctor and Find a Location functionality
  • Enhanced platform stability and performance
  • Responsive design optimization across all devices

Business Value

  • Successful platform stabilization during critical launch period
  • Improved patient engagement through enhanced Find Care tools
  • Streamlined appointment scheduling reducing administrative burden
  • Better healthcare provider discoverability
  • Enhanced patient portal experience through MyChart integration
  • Reduced support tickets through improved site functionality
