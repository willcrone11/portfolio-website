Vanderbilt Specialty Pharmacy

Component-Based Website Redesign & Scalable Architecture

Summary

Role: Lead Developer (6 months)

Live Site: vanderbiltspecialtypharmacy.com

Technologies: Drupal, Twig, PHP, Pantheon, GitHub, jQuery, SCSS

Led complete website redesign using atomic design principles and component-based architecture. Delivered project 2 months ahead of schedule, creating 20+ reusable components across 5 page templates. Built scalable component library designed for reuse across 500+ sites in Vanderbilt ecosystem, successfully addressing diverse user needs for patients, manufacturers, providers, and researchers.

Project Approach & Architecture

Atomic Design Methodology

Starting from the design level, our approach focused on atomic design principles and building a comprehensive component library rather than creating highly structured content types. This strategic decision prioritized flexibility and scalability, as the client valued the ability to reuse and reorder components across content types.

Component Library Development

Scalable Architecture: Built a collection of 5 page templates and 20+ reusable and reorderable custom components designed not only for the VSP site but for reuse across Vanderbilt's 500+ sites on their Barista platform (their Vanderbilt-specific Drupal instance).

Flexibility Focus: All components were architected to be reusable and reorderable across the site and other sites for maximum flexibility and scalability, enabling content editors to create diverse page layouts while maintaining design consistency.

Multi-Audience User Experience

The Vanderbilt Specialty Pharmacy serves a unique and diverse set of audiences, each with distinct needs and user journeys. Successfully designed and implemented solutions catering to:

  • Patients - Seeking prescription information, support, and pharmacy services
  • Manufacturers - Looking for partnership opportunities and distribution information
  • Providers - Requiring clinical information and prescribing resources
  • Researchers - Accessing data, studies, and collaboration opportunities

Development Process & Collaboration

Worked directly with the client on a daily basis in an agile environment to consult on and build out the VSP site. This close collaboration ensured that the final product met the specific needs of each user group while maintaining technical excellence and scalability.
