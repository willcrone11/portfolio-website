About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer based in Nashville, TN. Currently, I work as an Software Engineer at Modea. I have a broad knowledge of both back-end and front-end technologies and frameworks.

I have a background in classical music, having taken music lessons since the age of 3. In 2020, I graduated with a B.M. in French Horn performance from the Cleveland Institute of Music, one of the top conservatories in the country. My classical music background taught me the value of patience and hard work and helped me to develop my creative sensibilities.

I got my start as a developer working with JavaScript within the React framework. In my current role at Modea, I spend the majority of my time building large, patient-facing enterprise web projects for hospitals and healthcare systems; these are built primarily with either Drupal or Wordpress.

I am well-versed in Agile development and have a bevy of experience working in a team setting. In addition, as Modea is a consultancy, I am used to working with clients and stakeholders on a daily basis to ensure we are adapting to challenges and developing great products.

In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, playing the Horn and going on adventures with my dog Momo!
